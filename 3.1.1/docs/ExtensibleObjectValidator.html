ExtensibleObjectValidator

ExtensibleObjectValidator

Validates the requirements of an object. Verifiers and Validators contain corresponding methods. Some exceptions are thrown by both methods. The remaining exceptions that are thrown by the verifier are wrapped as validation failures and are returned by #getFailures.
